Different types of Apples

Apples are nutritious fruits, rich in fiber and vitamins, available in various flavors and colors. Great for snacking!

Granny Smith

Originating from Australia, these apples are bright green, with a tart flavor that makes them excellent for pies and sauces.

Honeycrisp

Developed in Minnesota, Honeycrisp apples are known for their exceptional juiciness and balance of sweetness and acidity, making them a popular choice for fresh eating.

Fuji

Created in Japan, Fuji apples are a cross between Red Delicious and Ralls Janet varieties. They are very sweet, have dense flesh, and a long shelf life, making them ideal for snacking and storage.
